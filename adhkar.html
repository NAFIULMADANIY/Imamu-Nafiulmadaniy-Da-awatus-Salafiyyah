Explore daily supplications (adhkar) from the Sunnah of the Prophet ﷺ.

🕋 Morning & Evening Adhkar

  • Subḥānallāh wa biḥamdih – 100x
  • Āyat al-Kursiyy – After each prayer and before sleep
  • Sayyid al-Istighfār – Morning and evening

🛌 Before Sleep

  • Recite Surah Al-Mulk and Surah As-Sajdah
  • Blow into your hands and recite Surah Ikhlas, Falaq, and Nas three times
  • Say: “Allāhumma bismika amūtu wa aḥyā”

🕌 After Salah

  • Astaghfirullāh (3x)
  • Allāhumma antas-salām…
  • 33x Subḥānallāh, 33x Alḥamdulillāh, 34x Allāhu Akbar

May Allah accept our supplications and remember us as we remember Him. Ameen.
