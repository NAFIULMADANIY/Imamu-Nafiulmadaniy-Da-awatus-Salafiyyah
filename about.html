📖 About This Da’wah Project

Welcome to Imamu Nafiulmadaniy Da’awatus Salafiyyah — a digital da’wah platform aimed at spreading authentic Islamic teachings to a global audience in Arabic, English, and Hausa.

  • 🌍 Vision: To make authentic Islamic knowledge accessible to every Muslim regardless of their location or language.
  • 📚 Methodology: Following the path of the righteous predecessors (Manhaj as-Salaf).
  • 🔒 Security: Fully protected with AI tools, 2FA, and ethical hacking support.
  • 🛠️ Built by: Nafi’u Aliyu Adam and team — with support from Imamu Nafiulmadaniy and Hafsat Muhammad Abubakar.

This website is part of a long-term legacy effort to preserve Islamic knowledge and protect Muslim families spiritually and digitally.
