Project Overview

Imamu Nafiulmadaniy Da'awatus Salafiyyah is a global Islamic da’wah platform designed to spread authentic Salafi teachings through multimedia content and secure admin contribution.

Languages Supported:

  • Arabic
  • English
  • Hausa

Objectives:

  • Educate with Qur’an, Sunnah, and Salafi scholarship
  • Enable structured da’wah delivery worldwide
  • Securely preserve and transfer legacy
